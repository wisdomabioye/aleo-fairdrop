# Claiming

After an auction closes and settles, you can claim your tokens or, if the auction was
voided, a full refund of your payment.

---

## When can you claim?

| Auction status | What you can claim |
|---|---|
| **Cleared** | Your won tokens (or a vesting record if vesting is enabled) |
| **Voided** | A full refund of your payment |

Auctions that are still **Active**, **Clearing**, or **Ended** are not yet claimable.

---

## Where to claim

Go to the **Claim** page (`/claim`). Connect your wallet — the page loads your bid records
from your wallet automatically and groups them by auction.
