# Bidding — Raise auction

---

## Step by step

1. Navigate to the auction detail page and connect your wallet.
2. Pass gate verification if required.
3. Select **Private** or **Public** bid mode.
4. Enter your **contribution amount in ALEO** (not a token quantity).
5. The form shows your contribution, the protocol fee, and your credit record balance
   (private mode).
6. For private bids: select a credit record.
7. Optionally enter a referral code.
8. Click **Contribute** and confirm in your wallet.

---

## Things to know

- You are contributing ALEO credits, not buying a fixed quantity of tokens.
- Your token allocation is determined at close: `supply × your_contribution / total_contributions`.
- If the raise target is exceeded, the effective price per token rises proportionally —
  you receive fewer tokens for the same contribution.
- Min and max contribution amounts are enforced if the creator set them.
- The Raise simulator on the auction page shows optimistic and conservative allocation
  estimates based on current total contributions.

---

## After contributing

Your contribution appears in **My Bids**. When the auction closes and settles, claim your
tokens from the **Claim** page.

See [Claiming tokens](../claiming/claim.md).
