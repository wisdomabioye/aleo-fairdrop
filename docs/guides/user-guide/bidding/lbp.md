# Bidding — LBP auction

---

## Step by step

1. Navigate to the auction detail page and connect your wallet.
2. Pass gate verification if required.
3. Select **Private** or **Public** bid mode.
4. Enter your **budget in ALEO**. The form shows how many tokens you would receive at the
   current price.
5. For private bids: select a credit record.
6. Optionally enter a referral code.
7. Click **Place Bid** and confirm in your wallet.

---

## Things to know

- You pay the price at the moment your transaction finalizes (pay-your-bid, not clearing).
- The price only decreases over time — there is no incentive to rush. Waiting typically
  yields more tokens for the same budget.
- LBP auctions do not support early close. The auction runs for its full duration.
- The LBP simulator on the auction page shows estimated tokens at the current price.

---

## After bidding

Your bid appears in **My Bids**. Claim your tokens after the auction ends.

See [Claiming tokens](../claiming/claim.md).
