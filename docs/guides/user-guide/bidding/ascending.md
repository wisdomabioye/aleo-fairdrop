# Bidding — Ascending auction

---

## Step by step

1. Navigate to the auction detail page and connect your wallet.
2. Pass gate verification if required.
3. Select **Private** or **Public** bid mode.
4. Enter the **quantity** of tokens you want.
5. The form shows the current price and total payment at that price.
6. For private bids: select a credit record.
7. Optionally enter a referral code.
8. Click **Place Bid** and confirm in your wallet.

---

## Things to know

- You pay the price **at the moment your transaction finalizes**. Unlike Dutch and Sealed,
  there is no uniform clearing price — each bidder pays their own bid price.
- Bidding earlier locks in a lower price, but supply may not be fully available to later
  bidders.
- If a bid is placed within the **extension window** before the scheduled end, the auction
  extends automatically. The form shows the current end block.
- Min and max bid amounts apply to the quantity field.

---

## After bidding

Your bid appears in **My Bids**. Claim your tokens after the auction closes.

See [Claiming tokens](../claiming/claim.md).
