# Step 6 — Referral

Your auction automatically participates in the Fairdrop referral system.
No configuration is required on this step — it is informational only.

---

## How referrals work

When a bidder places a bid using a referral link (generated by any Fairdrop user), the
referrer earns a commission. The commission is paid from the protocol fee at auction close —
not from the creator's proceeds.

```
Total payments at close
      │
      └── Protocol fee (% of total)
               │
               └── Referral pool (% of protocol fee)
                        │
                        └── Referrer commission (up to max % of referral pool)
```

The exact percentages are set by the protocol configuration and are shown on this step.
They are the same for all auctions.

---

## What this means for you as a creator

- You do not need to fund referral rewards separately.
- Referral commissions come out of the protocol fee — your net proceeds are not reduced
  beyond the standard fee.
- Referral links to your auction can be created by anyone from the auction detail page.
